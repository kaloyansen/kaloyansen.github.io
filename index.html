who am i ?

I am a software developer since 2000 with more than twenty years of experience on linux platforms. I have designed applications with command-line, web and graphical user interfaces. The source code is in multiple object-oriented programming languages, mainly python and C++. My recent work is dedicated to embedded operating systems and corresponding applications. Learn more in my CV.

I have learned to do precise measurements, experimental data analysis and online visualization during my education (a PhD in particle physics) and later in science and industry. In addition, I am an expert in open-source software. I have done a lot of technical writing and almost one hundred public presentations during my professional career in Bulgaria, Germany and France.
